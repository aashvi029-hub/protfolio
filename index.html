Blog Improvements

4 quick wins your blog is missing right now

1

Sticky CTA Button

A "Get Free Audit" or "Talk to Us" button that stays visible as the reader scrolls. Most blog readers leave without taking action, a sticky button fixes that.

2

Internal Linking

Blog posts aren't linking to each other or to your service pages. This hurts your Google rankings and stops readers from discovering more of your work.

3

AI Summary at the Top

Add a "Summarize in ChatGPT" option that redirects readers to ChatGPT with a ready-made prompt based on the blog content.

4

Audio Blog (Listen Mode)

Let visitors listen to your blog instead of reading. A small "Listen" button at the top, great for mobile users and builds a more premium experience.

5

FAQ Section on Every Blog

FAQs at the bottom help you rank for question-based searches on Google. They also help your content appear in AI answer engines, a simple add with big SEO payoff.

Website Improvements

Two changes that can directly increase trust and leads

Missing

Your AI Story

Right now, your website doesn't talk about how YOU use AI. That's a missed opportunity.

Add a section like:

"How We Use AI to Deliver Better Results Faster"

Show that you use AI tools for SEO research, campaign analysis, content, and reporting. Clients today want to work with agencies that are forward-thinking.

Opportunity

Video Testimonials on Service Page

You have 500+ brand success stories. But written testimonials are easy to fake.

What to do: Add 2–3 short video testimonials (30–60 seconds) from real clients on the service page. Also, display service-specific testimonials on relevant service pages (e.g., SEO testimonials on the SEO page, PPC testimonials on the PPC page)..

Impact: Video testimonials build trust 10x faster than text. They convert better and lower bounce rates.

Social Media Strategy

3 content ideas that position Webrex as an industry leader

01

Shopify + AI Updates

Be the first to talk about it

Every week, post a short update about what's new in Shopify or how AI is changing eCommerce. Position Webrex as the go-to source for what's happening in the industry.

"3 Shopify updates this week you need to know about 👀"
02

How We Use AI

Show your process, not just results

Behind-the-scenes content showing how your team uses AI for keyword research, reporting, ad copy, or CRO analysis. This builds credibility and makes potential clients feel they're getting the most advanced help available.

03

Case Studies with Strong Hook Lines

Lead with the number, then tell the story

Instead of just sharing a case study, open with a strong hook. People stop scrolling for specific, believable results.

"This brand was stuck at 0.2% conversion. 90 days later: 1.3%. Here's exactly what we changed 👇"
